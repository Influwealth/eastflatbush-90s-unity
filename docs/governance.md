# ⚖️ Digital Twin Governance: East Flatbush Project

## 1. Data Provenance
- **Base Geometry**: Sourced from NYC Open Data (PLUTO) and public building footprints.
- **Ownership**: The derivative 3D models and simulation insights are community-owned assets under the WealthBridge OS framework.

## 2. Privacy & Civil Rights (NY Law Compliance)
- **Visual Privacy**: This project uses stylized or generative textures. No unblurred faces or private interior residence data (PII) is permitted without express consent.
- **Pictorial Representation**: Compliance with the Architectural Works Copyright Protection Act (1990) for buildings visible from public spaces.

## 3. Sovereign Usage
- **Fair Use**: This project is for educational, civic, and urban planning purposes.
- **Ledger Transparency**: Economic simulations on the WealthBridge Ledger are anonymized to protect individual tenant financial data while tracking community-level value.
